the journey a bit about this project...

Notch helps people prepare better for their job interviews. It is a global platform that connects aspiring job seekers with experienced professionals from the industry, whom they can practice their interview with.We believe that practicing for a job interview with the right people and getting honest feedback significantly improves odds of getting in. Our algorithm matches candidates with the most suitable professional based on their profile, experience and their target firms.
